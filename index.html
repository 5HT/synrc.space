N2O Ecosystem

Synrc Space defines N2O, a full-stack specification and implementation for banking, messaging and website development. It specifies the way to scale applications, protocols, processes, services, events, and persistence. Ready for high-speed, low-latency IoT, MQTT, and WebSocket applications, N2O powers banks, messaging systems and several startups.
